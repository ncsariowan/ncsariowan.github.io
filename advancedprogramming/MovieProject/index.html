The Princess Bride

Directed by Rob Reiner

The Princess Bride centers around a fairy tale told by a grandfather to his grandson, about a princess named Buttercup and her lover, Westley. Westley, who had been believed to dead, comes to find Buttercup and take her back from King Humperdinck. A cult classic, The Princess Bride has been highly acclaimed by critics and audiences.
